## reComputer R1000 Configurator

This add-on continuously verifies and repairs reComputer R1000 specific settings after boot.

Focus areas:
- RS485 UART overlays and availability checks
- LED availability checks for led-red/led-green/led-blue
- Buzzer v1.1 readiness on GPIO591

The add-on is designed to stay installed and running, so settings are restored if they are overwritten by updates.
